Frequently Asked Questions
1. When should I schedule my first pregnancy appointment?
You should schedule your first pregnancy appointment as soon as you confirm your pregnancy, usually between 6 and 8 weeks after your last menstrual period.

4. Why is early prenatal care important?

5. How often will I need prenatal visits during pregnancy?
